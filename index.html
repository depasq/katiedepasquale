About Katie

Katie DePasquale is a freelance writer, editor, and project manager in the Boston area with a master's degree in writing and publishing from Emerson College. She enjoys telling a good story and making sure it's correctly punctuated, and monitoring other people's schedules.

Contact Katie!

At Your Service


Editing

Writing

Project Management


For about 15 years, I have worked as a writer, editor, and project manager for educational publishers, nonprofits, universities, marketing companies, and vendors. Part of that time was spent in-house at a large educational publisher in Boston. I'm comfortable juggling multiple jobs and deadlines while maintaining the quality and integrity of the content. I have written everything from blog posts for a personal injury lawyer's website to a profile of a research scientist studying robotics, edited content ranging from a spy novel to a manual on Git, and managed teams who have planned an international healthcare systems excellence workshop and who have written a series of bestselling math textbooks.

As a writer, in addition to my work I enjoy writing fiction and personal essays. Conducting interviews and weaving them into engaging personal profiles is one of my favorite parts of my job. You can access selections from my writing portfolio here [LINK]. As an editor, I am well-versed in the major style guides including CMS and APA, and I'm comfortable learning and implementing new house styles quickly and accurately. Planning and maintaining schedules, reviewing the elements of graphic design, and fostering positive communication are among my strengths as a project manager.

Please feel free to contact me for more information or to discuss the particulars of your project.
